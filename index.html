Turnstr is an online mobile photos and videos sharing app.
It enables user to take pictures and videos, and post them either publicly or privately on the app and also on variety of other social networking platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, and Flickr. This is an app with a difference where user can swipe to rotate (3d view) to see 4 images.
